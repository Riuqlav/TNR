The Origin Story

I.

I was once told that I should write it all down. The man who told me so, was viewed as a hero by many, a survivor of a great war, who came for the rescue of his long – lost brethren. This hero is long gone now, but his words never allowed me to rest. Today, I will tell you my story, as it may serve you in the very near future. Please, do not take my words for granted, for they are born from a journey much longer than my days on this Earth, marked by a destruction too great for anyone to bear.

II.

Likewise, the love, the pride, and the sense of hope that I carry within me, are what raise my spirit today. For I see the end of my exile, and with it, the re-edification of my throne, my House, and my People – which is necessary to happen in this exact order. However, as you read these words, I have a simple request; that you let go of all skepticism, that you value my sincerity, and that you hold my narrative close to your heart. Remember; all that is here, is to be shared only with those who also seek the truth.

III.

Yes, I do believe it is no accident that you have come across this manuscript, as this was also foretold by the Elders (or so I remember).

IV.

I don’t know who or where you are, but I was assured that whoever reads this, does have a share in the Kingdom of the Greater Realm. No. I am not a mystic, so I cannot sense how these writings will resonate with you. But apparently, “two will read the same message, and only one will understand its truth.” I understand this sounds odd, but it is what I was told — as this is a truth I also seek. Indeed, my role in the coming era is important, but I am not at all one who acts by spiritual insight. Instead, only by the instructions of those who wish for the return of my presence in the midst of my People.

V.

As I bring up certain words, such as those in reference to a past kingdom, please do not think of me as anyone of any might or power today; as my current condition is one of exile, distress, and solitude. I have no shelter from the elements, no security of body, and no surplus of any kind. Today, I simply wander through the ruins of what once was… But for some reason, even those ruins, seem to be just enough for the flame of my soul to keep on burning — although dimly.

VI.

As I wander through the ruins, memories of a glorious past flood my mind. I see the grand halls and towering spires that once stood proud, now reduced to rubble and decay. The echoes of laughter and music that once filled these halls are replaced by the haunting silence of abandonment.

I trace the path of destruction with heavy steps, my heart burdened with the weight of loss. It was not always like this. Once, my kingdom flourished with prosperity, its people united under a common purpose. But a darkness befell us—an enemy from within—who sowed seeds of discord and greed.

I remember the day it all changed—the day the usurper seized the throne and cast me into exile. My people, blinded by false promises and seduced by the illusion of power, turned their backs on me. Betrayed and alone, I fled to the farthest corners of the realm, seeking solace in anonymity.

For years, I lived as a mere shadow, observing the struggles of my people from afar. I watched as they suffered under the rule of tyranny, their spirits crushed and their hopes extinguished. But in their darkest hour, whispers of rebellion began to surface.

It was then that the Elders found me, their ancient wisdom guiding them to the rightful heir. They revealed the prophecies that spoke of my return, the restoration of balance and justice. It was their belief in my destiny that rekindled the flickering flame within me.

VII.

The path ahead is treacherous, filled with danger and uncertainty. But I am no longer the naïve prince who was banished from his throne. I have witnessed the depths of despair and the resilience of the human spirit. I have learned that true power lies not in the hands of a single ruler, but in the collective will of a united people.

Gathering my strength, I vow to reclaim what is rightfully mine. The time for hiding and contemplation is over. It is time to rally my loyal allies, to inspire the downtrodden, and to ignite the flames of hope in their hearts.

As I continue my journey, I carry with me the stories of my ancestors, the tales of their triumphs and failures. Their legacy flows through my veins, empowering me to face the challenges that lie ahead. I am determined to build a kingdom that stands not just as a symbol of my lineage but as a beacon of light for all who seek justice and freedom.

To you, dear reader, I extend an invitation. Join me on this arduous yet necessary path. Stand with me in the face of adversity, for the restoration of my throne is but a part of a greater struggle—a fight against the forces of darkness that threaten to consume us all.

Will you heed the call and be counted among those who dare to dream of a better future? The choice is yours.
